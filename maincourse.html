Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb
    • This elegant dish features a succulent rack of lamb encrusted with a fragrant blend of fresh herbs, garlic, and breadcrumbs, then roasted to a perfect medium-rare. The herb crust creates a flavorful and crispy exterior while the lamb remains tender and juicy inside. Served with a rich red wine reduction and accompanied by creamy mashed potatoes and seasonal roasted vegetables, this main course offers a sophisticated and satisfying dining experience.

  • Lemon Basil Chicken with Sun-Dried Tomato Orzo
    • Juicy chicken breasts are marinated in a zesty lemon and basil sauce, then grilled or baked to perfection. The chicken is served atop a bed of orzo pasta tossed with sun-dried tomatoes, fresh basil, and a touch of Parmesan cheese. The combination of bright lemon and aromatic basil enhances the chicken, while the orzo provides a flavorful, hearty accompaniment. This dish is both vibrant and comforting, ideal for a refined yet hearty meal.

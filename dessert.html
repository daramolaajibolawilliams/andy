  • Salted Caramel Panna Cotta
    • This creamy Italian classic features a velvety, vanilla-infused panna cotta with a luscious salted caramel sauce drizzled on top. The panna cotta is chilled to perfection, offering a smooth, silky texture that melts in your mouth. The rich caramel adds a sweet and salty contrast, finished with a light sprinkle of sea salt for an extra touch of sophistication.

  • Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake Bars
    • These cheesecake bars combine a tangy lemon flavor with juicy, fresh blueberries for a refreshing twist on a classic dessert. The creamy cheesecake filling is set atop a buttery graham cracker crust, and each bar is swirled with blueberry compote, giving it a beautiful marbled effect. A touch of lemon zest and a dusting of powdered sugar complete the bars, making them a delightful treat with a perfect balance of tart and sweet.
